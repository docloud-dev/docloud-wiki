# How to create a Docloud Developer Account.

1. **Request Access:**
   - Go to [dof.docloud.lk](http://dof.docloud.lk/).
   - Request access to a developer account by either filling out a form or asking your system administrator to create one for you.
2. **Check Email:**
   - Once your request is processed, check your email for a welcome message.
   - The email will contain a set-up link.
3. **Set-up:**
   - Click on the activation link to set up your account.
   - Follow the prompts to create a password for your account.
4. **Login to Docloud Dev System:**
   - After setting up your password, you will be redirected to the Docloud developer system.
   - Congratulations! You now have access to your Docloud Developer Account.

<aside>
💡 Please be aware that the setup link has a 15-minute expiration period.

</aside>

# How to add the app to DoCloud

1. **Login to DoCloud:**

   Access your DoCloud account by logging in with your developer credentials.

2. **Navigate to App Library:**

   Once logged in, locate and navigate to the "App Library" section. This is usually accessible from the sidebar or a designated menu.

3. **Upload App File:**

   Look for a button to add an app to the library. Choose Add an app to the library, and then select the app file you previously exported from your DoFramework. This is typically a file with a zip extension.

4. **Confirm the App:**

   Review the information about your app that is displayed. Confirm the details and click the "Add App" button to complete the process.

   By following these steps, you should be able to successfully add your app to DoCloud. Ensure that you provide any necessary information or configuration settings during the process and double-check the app details before confirming. This will make your app available for use within the DoCloud platform.
